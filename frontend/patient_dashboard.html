🛡️ How Your Records Are Protected
1
Data encrypted with AES-256-GCM — a unique random 256-bit key per record
2
Key exchanged via ECDH SECP384R1 — only you & authorised doctors can derive it
3
SHA-256 fingerprint computed — any tampering immediately invalidates the hash
4
Hash signed with ECDSA SECP256K1 — cryptographically proves who created the record
5
Only the encrypted blob is stored — your plaintext never hits the database
